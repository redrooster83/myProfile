Before my career

Originally from a small city called East London in South Africa, from an early age I showed a keen interest in learning how to use a computer, even though the computers back then were 386k processors, but that's where I got my first taste for the Command Line.
During my teen years I relocated with my family to Johannesburg in South Africa where I would go on to complete a BTech in Office Administration.
Later in my teen years I started using a chat program called iRC and learnt that it was a puTTy tool connecting to a Linux server using a scripting language which I began decoding. I would later figure out how to customise the script and thus began my love for learning how all machines and programs worked.
I worked at various temping jobs and other roles, but constantly in my spare time in which ever way was possible, learnt various IT skills including Linux, Virtualisation, SQL, HTML, PC Hardware Repairs and Peripherals, PHP, XML and Python
